Boolean
a boolean is a data type that represents one of two possible values: true or false. Booleans are commonly used for logical operations and decisions in programming. They're fundamental for controlling the flow of execution in programs through conditional statements like if-else and while loops. Booleans can also be combined using logical operators such as AND, OR, and NOT to perform more complex decision-making.
